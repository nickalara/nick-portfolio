About Me

If that sounds all-over-the-place, I would rather it be a testament to my ability to work with a wide range of technologies and to learn quickly. By middle school I was hacking the family's home computer to bypass the parental controls and use the system freely. In high school I learned how to edit my MySpace page by injecting my profile with HTML and CSS. I've built and modified dozens of computers for myself, friends, and family.
